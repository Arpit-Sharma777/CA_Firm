Our Work

We deliver comprehensive financial solutions that drive business growth and ensure regulatory compliance through innovative technology and expert knowledge.

Our Core Services

Company Incorporation

Get up and running in days. We handle all the paperwork so you can focus on building your business.

What we do:

  • Helping you choose the right structure (Pvt. Ltd., LLP, OPC, etc.)
  • Reserving and approving your company name
  • Procuring digital signatures and director IDs
  • Filing incorporation forms (SPICe+, AGILE, etc.)
  • Setting up your PAN, TAN, bank account and statutory registers

GST Compliance

Stay on top of your tax filings. We make sure your GST returns are accurate and on time.

What we do:

  • New registrations and migration to the right GST scheme
  • Monthly/quarterly return preparation (GSTR-1, 3B, 4, 9, etc.)
  • E-invoicing and e-way-bill management
  • Credit and liability reconciliation reports
  • Representation in assessments, notices and appeals

ROC (MCA) Filings

Avoid penalties—meet every deadline. We keep your Ministry of Corporate Affairs filings up to date.

What we do:

  • Annual financials (AOC-4) and returns (MGT-7)
  • Director and office-change filings (DIR-12, INC-22)
  • Share allotment and charge registration (PAS-3, CHG forms)
  • LLP-specific returns (Form 11, Form 8)
  • Director KYC and office-address KYC

Income-Tax Return Filing

Maximise savings, minimise stress. Accurate returns, on time — for individuals, firms and companies.

What we do:

  • TDS/TCS return filing and certificate issuance
  • Tax audit (Form 3CD) and transfer-pricing documentation
  • Preparation and e-filing of returns for all entity types
  • Advance-tax planning and installment filings
  • Handling notices, assessments and refunds

Auditing & Assurance

Build trust with your stakeholders. Objective reviews, detailed reports — from statutory to internal audits.

What we do:

  • Statutory audits under Companies Act and GST
  • Internal, operational and risk-based audits
  • Concurrent and bank audits for lenders
  • Inventory (stock) and CAG-style audits
  • Due-diligence for investments, M&A and certifications

Project Reports & Finance Proposals

Turn your idea into funded reality. Crystal-clear reports that lenders and investors love.

What we do:

  • Detailed feasibility (technical, financial, economic)
  • Six-year CMA data packs with ratio and cash-flow forecasts
  • Loan proposals and presentation decks for banks/NBFCs
  • Government scheme and subsidy applications

Trademark Services

Protect your brand for the long haul. From searches to renewals, we safeguard your identity.

What we do:

  • Comprehensive availability search and class advice
  • Filing and prosecution of trademark applications
  • Handling oppositions and cancellations
  • Renewal, assignment, licensing and watch services

Bookkeeping & Accounting

Clear, up-to-date books—every month. Reliable numbers to drive decisions and keep you audit-ready.

What we do:

  • Setting up your choice of accounting software
  • Daily bookkeeping: journals, ledgers and bank reconciliation
  • Payroll processing with PF/ESI/PT calculations
  • Monthly management accounts and dashboards
  • Budgeting, cash-flow planning and variance analysis
